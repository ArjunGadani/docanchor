# Acme Corp Leave Policy (Effective January 1, 2026)

## Leave Entitlements
Full-time employees are entitled to the following paid leave per calendar year:
- **Casual leave:** 12 days per year, accrued at 1 day per month.
- **Sick leave:** 10 days per year. A medical certificate is required for any sick leave of more than 2 consecutive days.
- **Earned (privilege) leave:** 18 days per year, available after the 90-day probation period is completed.

Part-time employees accrue leave on a pro-rata basis according to their contracted hours.

## Carry-Forward and Encashment
- Unused **casual leave** may carry forward up to a maximum of 30 days. Casual leave is **not** encashable.
- Unused **earned leave** carries forward without an upper cap, but only up to 45 days may be encashed at the time of resignation or retirement.
- **Sick leave** does not carry forward and lapses at the end of the calendar year.

## Applying for Leave
Casual and earned leave must be applied for through the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance. Leave of more than 5 consecutive days requires 2 weeks' advance notice and explicit manager approval. Sick leave should be reported to the manager by 10:00 AM on the first day of absence.

All leave requests are routed to the reporting manager for approval. If a manager does not respond within 2 working days, the request is auto-escalated to the next-level manager.

## Public Holidays
Acme Corp observes 12 public holidays per calendar year. Of these, 8 are fixed company-wide holidays and 4 are floating holidays that each employee may choose from an approved regional list. Floating holidays must be used within the calendar year and cannot be carried forward.

## Parental Leave
- **Maternity leave:** 26 weeks of fully paid leave, which may begin up to 8 weeks before the expected due date.
- **Paternity leave:** 4 weeks of fully paid leave, to be taken within 6 months of the child's birth or adoption.
- **Adoption leave:** 12 weeks of fully paid leave for the primary caregiver.

## Unpaid and Special Leave
Leave without pay (LWOP) of up to 30 days per year may be granted at management's discretion once all paid leave is exhausted. Bereavement leave of 5 days is provided for an immediate family member.

# Acme Corp Employee Handbook (2026 Edition)

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday, with a one-hour lunch break. The core collaboration window, during which all employees must be available, is 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Total expected working time is 40 hours per week.

Employees may use flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:30 AM, provided they complete 8 working hours and are present during the core window. Overtime beyond 45 hours in a week must be pre-approved in writing by the reporting manager.

## Remote and Hybrid Work
Acme Corp follows a hybrid model. All employees are required to work from the office a minimum of 3 days per week. Tuesdays and Thursdays are designated mandatory in-office "anchor days" for the whole company.

Fully remote arrangements are available only with VP-level approval and are reviewed every 6 months. Employees working remotely must be reachable on Slack within 30 minutes during core hours and maintain an internet connection of at least 25 Mbps.

## Probation Period
New full-time employees serve a probation period of 90 days from their start date. During probation, either party may end the employment with 7 days' notice. Probation may be extended once, by up to 30 additional days, at the manager's discretion with HR approval.

## Compensation and Payroll
Salaries are paid monthly on the last working day of each month. Payslips are available on the HR portal by the 1st of the following month. The annual salary review cycle concludes in April, with revisions effective from May 1.

Reimbursements (travel, internet, equipment) must be submitted with receipts by the 5th of the month to be included in that month's payroll; late submissions roll over to the next cycle.

## Benefits Overview
- **Health insurance:** Company-funded coverage of up to $8,000 per year for the employee, spouse, and up to two children. Coverage begins on the first day of employment (no waiting period).
- **Provident fund:** The company contributes 12% of base salary, matched by an equal employee contribution.
- **Annual learning budget:** $1,200 per employee per calendar year for courses, books, and conferences. Unused budget does not carry forward.
- **Wellness stipend:** $50 per month toward gym or wellness memberships.
- **Equipment:** A laptop refresh is offered every 3 years.

## Contact and Escalation
For handbook questions, contact the People Operations team at people@acme.example. Policy disputes are escalated first to the reporting manager, then to the HR Business Partner, and finally to the Head of People.
